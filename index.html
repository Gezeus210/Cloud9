Nissan Skyline

What is a Nissan Skyline

The Nissan Skyline is a very reliable and fast racing sports car that was only sold domestically in Japan, however some buyers have been lucky enough to import certain models into the United States. It is an infamous car partly due to the fact that the car appeared in the popular street racing movie,"2 Fast 2 Furious".

What kind of engine does a Skyline use?

The majority of nissan skylines use an In-line six cylinder engine.

What is an In-line six ? An "in-line six" engine is one that contains six cylinders but going in a straight line rather than taking the other usual shape of the v6 engine
